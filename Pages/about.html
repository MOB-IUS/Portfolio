Hello, you've probably already learned something about me, but I’ll tell you more.

My name is Yuchen Liu and I go by Eric. I am a third-year transfer student majoring in Game Design and Interactive Media at University of california, Irvine. My goal is to become a game designer and developer in the future. For the two years before I transferred, I was a Computer Science student at University of California, Santa Barbara. Thanks for this, I’ve developed solid skills in programming with both C++ and C#, and is a fast learner in the programming side.

Though it’s my first year enrolled in game development, I’ve learned basic knowledge in aspects including but not limited to game design, visual design, storytelling in games, and game society. Beside all these, I myself am a game lover. Some of my favorite games include the Octopath Traveler series, Persona 5, and Terraria.

I am building this portfolio website not only to advertise myself, but also to keep a record of myself in this path I’ve chosen. Hopefully, you’ve learned something about me :)
